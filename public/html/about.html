Beat-Collab is website focused on bringing a collaborative approach to drum beat creation. Inspired by a web development class in college, developer Mitchell Nelson started the project during spring of 2019 and continues to improve to site with design upgrades and feature integration.

Sounds

All drum samples have been provided by Black Octopus Sound in their free sample pack. This sample pack and many other premium sounds can be found on their website.
